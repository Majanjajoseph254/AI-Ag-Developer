3. **Features**

**A. User Management**

**D. Soil & Erosion Control**

**E. Market Linkage**
   - Show nearest markets using geolocation

   - Contact buyers directly

**F. AI Chatbot**
   - Chatbot integrated using OpenAI GPT API
   - Answer agronomy questions
   - Store chat history in DB

     - "How do I treat maize leaf blight?"

**G. Weather & Flood Alerts**

   - Optional: IoT or drone integration for large farms
   - Offline mode: save inputs offline and sync later
